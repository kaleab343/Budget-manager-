# BudgetManager - Your Personal Finance Tracker


## Description

BagetManager is a user-friendly Android application designed to empower you to take control of your financial life.  Effortlessly track your expenses, gain insights into your spending habits, and achieve your budgeting goals. This app provides a simple and intuitive interface for managing your money on the go.

## Key Features

*   **Easy Expense Logging:** Quickly add new expenses with just a few taps.
*   **Categorized Spending:**  Assign categories to each expense (e.g., Groceries, Travel, Utilities) for detailed analysis.
*   **Budgeting Made Simple:** Set a monthly budget and monitor your progress in real-time. See how much you've spent and how much you have left.
*   **Clear Data Presentation:** (Future Enhancement) Visualize your spending with informative charts and graphs to identify trends.
*   **Monthly Expense Reset:** Automatically clears expense data at the beginning of each month, providing a fresh start for your budget tracking.
*   **Offline Functionality:** Track your expenses even without an internet connection.

## Technologies & Libraries

*   **Android SDK:** For native Android app development.
*   **Java:** The primary programming language used.
*   **SQLite:** A lightweight, embedded database for storing expense and budget data locally on the device.
*   **Android SharedPreferences:** Used for storing user preferences and app settings.

## Getting Started

Follow these steps to get BagetManager up and running on your local machine:

 **Clone the Repository:**

1.  **Open with Android Studio:** Launch Android Studio and open the cloned project directory.

2.  **Build and Run:** Connect an Android device or start an emulator, then build and run the app from Android Studio.
